Love Poems for You

My Heart's Desire

In the garden of my heart, you bloom,
A flower of rare beauty and perfume.
Your petals soft as morning light,
Your essence pure, a wondrous sight.

With every dawn, my love grows strong,
For you, my dear, where I belong.
In your embrace, I find my home,
With you, I'll never be alone.

So on this day, I dare propose,
A life together, come what may oppose.
For you're the one my heart desires,
My love for you, it never tires.

Eternal Love

Like stars that shine in midnight sky,
Our love will sparkle, never die.
Through storms and calm, through day and night,
You are my anchor, my guiding light.

With every breath, with every beat,
My heart for you does softly speak.
Of promises and dreams so true,
Of endless love, just me and you.

So let us walk this path as one,
Our journey's only just begun.
Hand in hand, heart to heart,
Forever love, never to part.
